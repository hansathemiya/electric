Frequently Asked Questions About Six7 Electrical

How long has Six7 Electrical been in business?
Six7 Electrical was founded in 2018 and has been serving Colombo residents and businesses with emergency and planned electrical services ever since. We have grown from a single technician to a team of 14 licensed professionals while maintaining our commitment to fast, reliable service.
Are your electricians licensed and insured?
Yes. All Six7 Electrical technicians hold valid electrical licenses recognized in Sri Lanka and carry comprehensive public liability insurance. We maintain these credentials not just for compliance, but because your safety and protection are our priority.
What areas do you serve?
We provide emergency and scheduled electrical services across all Colombo postal districts (01-15) and surrounding suburbs including Rajagiriya, Nugegoda, Battaramulla, Dehiwala, Mount Lavinia, Kirulapone, Narahenpita, Borella, Malabe, and Maharagama. We also offer limited coverage in Gampaha and Kalutara districts – call +94 75 824 4216 to confirm availability for your location.
Do you handle both residential and commercial electrical work?
Yes. We provide full-service electrical solutions for homes and businesses. Our residential services include emergency repairs, wiring, solar, EV charging, and smart home automation. Our commercial services cover retail fit-outs, office rewiring, warehouse lighting, 3-phase installations, and preventive maintenance contracts.
How do you ensure quality and customer satisfaction?
Every job includes a pre-work safety assessment, transparent quote approval, post-work testing, and a 90-day workmanship guarantee. We follow up within 48 hours of job completion to ensure satisfaction, and we actively seek customer feedback to improve our services.
What makes Six7 Electrical different from other electricians in Colombo?
Our combination of true 24/7 emergency response with no call-out fee, transparent upfront pricing, deep local Colombo expertise, full-service capability, and customer-first communication sets us apart. We are locally owned, licensed, insured, and committed to earning your trust on every job.
