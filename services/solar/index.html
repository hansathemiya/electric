Net Metering Support – Get Credit for Excess Solar Production

Sri Lanka's net metering program allows solar system owners to export excess energy to the grid and receive credit on electricity bills. We provide end-to-end support for the CEB net metering application process.

Net Metering Process

  • 1. Eligibility Assessment – Verify property qualifies for net metering
  • 2. Application Preparation – Complete documentation and forms
  • 3. Submission and Coordination – CEB submission and follow-up
  • 4. Approval and Commissioning – Final system configuration and meter setup

Net Metering Benefits

  • Credit for excess production exported to grid
  • Reduced payback period for solar investment
  • No battery required for bill savings
  • Contribute renewable energy to Sri Lanka's grid

Net metering approval timelines vary based on CEB processing capacity. System installation can proceed while application processes; credits begin upon approval. We assess eligibility during initial consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions – Solar Power Installation

How much money will I save with solar panels in Colombo?
Savings depend on your system size, electricity usage, and tariff structure. A typical 3kW residential system in Colombo can reduce monthly electricity bills by 60-80%, with net metering credits providing additional savings. Most homeowners see full payback within 4-6 years, followed by decades of reduced or eliminated electricity costs. We provide personalized savings projections during consultation.
Do I need battery storage with my solar system?
Not necessarily. Grid-tied systems without batteries provide maximum ROI through net metering credits. Battery storage adds cost but provides backup power during outages and load-shedding. We help you decide based on your outage frequency, critical load needs, and budget priorities.
Will solar panels work during monsoon season or cloudy days?
Yes. Solar panels produce electricity whenever there is daylight, though production is reduced during heavy cloud cover or rain. Colombo's tropical climate provides sufficient annual sunshine for strong solar production. We design systems based on realistic production estimates that account for seasonal weather variations.
Do you handle CEB net metering applications?
Yes. We provide end-to-end support for CEB net metering applications including eligibility assessment, documentation preparation, application submission, and coordination with CEB representatives. We guide you through the entire process to maximize your solar investment return.
How long does solar panel installation take?
Typical residential installations take 2-5 days from start to commissioning. Commercial installations vary based on system scale, typically 5-14 days. Net metering application processing through CEB may take 2-8 weeks, but system installation can proceed while the application processes.
What maintenance do solar panels require?
Solar panels require minimal maintenance. We recommend occasional cleaning to remove dust or debris that may reduce production, and annual professional inspections to verify electrical connections and system performance. We offer maintenance contracts for ongoing care and warranty support.
What happens to my solar system if I move or sell my property?
Solar systems typically increase property value and can be transferred to new owners. We provide documentation supporting system ownership transfer and warranty continuation. Net metering accounts can also be transferred with CEB approval. We advise on optimal timing and documentation for property transactions involving solar systems.
