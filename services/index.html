Frequently Asked Questions – Electrical Services

What is the difference between emergency and standard service rates?
Emergency service (LKR 3,500/hour) applies to urgent issues requiring immediate attention: power outages, sparks, breaker tripping, storm damage. Standard service (LKR 2,500/hour) applies to planned, non-urgent work scheduled at your convenience. Emergency service has no call-out fee; standard service has a LKR 1,500 diagnostic fee waived if you proceed with repair.
Do you offer fixed-price quotes instead of hourly billing?
Yes. For defined-scope work like outlet installation, fixture replacement, or small projects, we can provide a fixed-price quote upon request. For larger or uncertain-scope jobs, hourly billing ensures you only pay for actual work performed.
Can you handle both residential and commercial electrical work?
Yes. We provide full-service electrical solutions for homes and businesses. Our residential team handles wiring, upgrades, and safety improvements. Our commercial team specializes in office rewiring, retail fit-outs, warehouse lighting, and maintenance contracts.
Are you certified to install solar panels and EV chargers?
Yes. Six7 Electrical technicians are trained and certified for solar PV installation, grid-tie systems, net metering applications, and EV charging infrastructure compliant with Sri Lankan standards.
What areas do you serve?
We provide emergency and scheduled electrical services across all Colombo postal districts (01-15) and surrounding suburbs including Rajagiriya, Nugegoda, Battaramulla, Dehiwala, Mount Lavinia, and more. Call +94 75 824 4216 to confirm coverage for your specific location.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes. All repairs and installations come with a 90-day workmanship guarantee. Parts carry manufacturer warranties (typically 1-24 months). If the same issue reoccurs within 90 days due to our work, we will return and correct it at no additional labor cost.
