Residential Electrical Issues We Resolve Daily

These common wiring problems affect many Colombo homes. If you recognize any of these issues, contact us for a professional assessment.

Flickering or Dimming Lights

Symptoms: Lights flicker when appliances turn on, dim unexpectedly

Common Causes: Loose connections, overloaded circuits, failing switches, outdated wiring

Urgency: Medium – schedule within 1-2 weeks

Outlets That Do Not Work

Symptoms: No power, work intermittently, trip breakers when used

Common Causes: Loose wiring, failed outlet, tripped GFCI, circuit overload

Urgency: Medium to High – schedule promptly

Frequent Breaker Tripping

Symptoms: Breaker trips repeatedly, especially when using certain appliances

Common Causes: Overloaded circuit, short circuit, ground fault, failing breaker

Urgency: High – schedule within 24-48 hours

Burning Smell or Warm Outlets

Symptoms: Burning odor from outlets, outlets feel warm, discoloration

Common Causes: Overloaded outlet, loose connection, failing component, fire hazard

Emergency – Call +94 75 824 4216 immediately

Sparks or Arcing from Outlets

Symptoms: Visible sparks when plugging in devices, popping sounds, intermittent power

Common Causes: Loose wiring, damaged outlet, short circuit, serious fire risk

Emergency – Call +94 75 824 4216 immediately

Outdated or Unsafe Wiring Systems

Symptoms: Home built before 1980, frequent issues, insurance requirements

Common Causes: Knob-and-tube wiring, aluminum wiring, insufficient grounding

Planning – schedule consultation for long-term solution

Modern Safety Features for Your Home Electrical System

Beyond basic wiring repairs, we offer advanced safety upgrades that protect your home, family, and electronics.

Whole-Home Surge Protection

Installation of surge protection devices at your main distribution board to protect against power spikes.

Benefits: Protects sensitive electronics, extends appliance lifespan, reduces fire risk

Ideal For: Homes with computers, entertainment systems, frequent power fluctuations

Typical Investment: LKR 15,000-35,000 depending on complexity

RCD/GFCI Outlet Installation

Replacement of standard outlets with Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter (GFCI) outlets in wet or high-risk areas.

Benefits: Prevents electrocution, meets modern safety codes, protects against ground faults

Ideal For: Kitchens, bathrooms, outdoor outlets, garages, basements

Typical Investment: LKR 3,500-8,000 per outlet

Arc-Fault Circuit Interrupter (AFCI) Protection

Installation of AFCI breakers or outlets that detect dangerous electrical arcs and interrupt power.

Benefits: Prevents electrical fires, enhances safety in bedrooms and living areas

Ideal For: Older homes, bedrooms, living areas, homes with children or elderly

Typical Investment: LKR 8,000-20,000 per circuit

Smart Electrical Panel Upgrade

Replacement of outdated distribution board with modern smart panel featuring remote monitoring and control.

Benefits: Real-time energy monitoring, remote circuit control, automated alerts

Ideal For: Tech-savvy homeowners, energy-conscious families, solar/backup power homes

Typical Investment: LKR 45,000-120,000 depending on features

Dedicated Circuit Installation

Addition of dedicated circuits for high-power appliances to prevent overloading and improve performance.

Benefits: Prevents breaker tripping, improves appliance performance, enhances system capacity

Ideal For: Home offices, kitchens with multiple appliances, workshops, entertainment rooms

Typical Investment: LKR 12,000-35,000 per circuit depending on complexity

Frequently Asked Questions – Residential Wiring

How long does a full house rewiring take?
Full house rewiring typically takes 2-7 days depending on home size, wiring complexity, and accessibility. We provide a detailed timeline during the quoting process and work to minimize disruption to your household routine.
Will rewiring damage my walls or finishes?
We use minimally invasive techniques whenever possible, including fishing wires through existing cavities and using existing access points. When new openings are necessary, we repair and repaint to match existing finishes. For heritage homes, we collaborate with conservation specialists to preserve architectural character.
Do I need to be home during wiring work?
For safety and access reasons, we require an adult to be present during wiring work. We can coordinate with your schedule and provide daily progress updates. For multi-day projects, we can discuss key access arrangements if needed.
What permits or approvals are required for rewiring?
Most residential wiring work does not require municipal permits in Colombo. However, full rewiring or major electrical upgrades may require notification to your insurance provider. We handle all compliance documentation and can coordinate with authorities if permits are needed.
How do I know if my home needs rewiring?
Signs your home may need rewiring include: frequent breaker tripping, flickering lights, outlets that do not work, burning smells, homes built before 1980, or insurance requirements. We offer comprehensive electrical safety inspections to assess your system and provide recommendations.
Can you work around my furniture and belongings?
Yes. We take care to protect your home during wiring work, including moving furniture with your guidance, using drop cloths, and cleaning up debris daily. For extensive projects, we can discuss temporary relocation of belongings if needed.
What warranty do you provide on wiring work?
All residential wiring services include a 90-day workmanship guarantee. If the same issue reoccurs within 90 days due to our work, we will return and correct it at no additional labor cost. Parts carry manufacturer warranties (typically 1-24 months).
