Frequently Asked Questions – Contacting Us

What is the fastest way to get help for an electrical emergency?
Call our 24/7 emergency line at +94 75 824 4216. This connects you directly to our dispatch team who can assess urgency and send the nearest technician immediately. WhatsApp is also monitored for emergencies but phone calls receive the fastest routing.
How do I know if my inquiry is an emergency or can wait?
Emergencies include: complete power outage not caused by CEB, burning smell or sparks from outlets, breaker tripping repeatedly, storm or flood damage to electrical systems, or any situation posing immediate fire or electrocution risk. If unsure, call us – we would rather assess and advise than have you wait with a potentially dangerous issue.
Can I schedule a non-emergency service for a specific date?
Yes. When booking via phone, WhatsApp, or our contact form, indicate your preferred date and time window (morning, afternoon, evening). We will confirm availability within 2 hours during office hours. For urgent non-emergency needs, mention "priority scheduling" and we will do our best to accommodate.
Do you offer quotes over the phone or email?
For simple, well-defined services (e.g., outlet installation, fixture replacement), we can provide ball park estimates over the phone. For complex or uncertain-scope work, we provide written quotes after a brief site assessment to ensure accuracy. Commercial projects always receive detailed written quotes after scope definition.
What information should I have ready when I contact you?
For fastest service: your full address or landmark, contact phone number, brief description of the electrical issue or service needed, and preferred contact method. For commercial inquiries: business name, property type, scope description, and timeline expectations.
Can I change or cancel a scheduled appointment?
Yes. Contact us at least 4 hours before your scheduled time to reschedule or cancel without penalty. For emergency appointments already en route, cancellation policies apply – see our Terms of Service for details.
How do I reach the commercial team specifically?
Call +94 75 824 4216 and ask for the commercial coordinator, or email electrical@six7.lk with "Commercial Inquiry" in the subject line. You may also mention "Commercial" in your first WhatsApp message for routing to the appropriate team member.
