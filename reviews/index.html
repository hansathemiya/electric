Frequently Asked Questions About Our Reviews

Learn how we collect, verify, and manage customer reviews to ensure authenticity and transparency.

How do you verify that reviews are from real customers?
We use multiple verification methods: Google reviews are verified through Google accounts, WhatsApp reviews require phone number verification, and form submissions require email confirmation. We cross-reference service records to ensure reviewers were actual customers. Reviews that cannot be verified are not published.
Do you remove negative reviews?
No. We believe in transparency and publish all verified reviews, including constructive criticism. We respond professionally to negative reviews to address concerns and demonstrate our commitment to customer satisfaction. If a review contains false information or violates our guidelines, we may request platform moderation but do not remove reviews arbitrarily.
How long does it take for my review to appear on your website?
Google reviews appear automatically via API integration within 24 hours. Testimonials submitted via our form are manually reviewed and published within 3 business days after verification. WhatsApp reviews are published after explicit permission is granted, typically within 48 hours.
Can I edit or delete my review after submitting it?
For Google reviews, you can edit or delete your review directly through your Google account. For testimonials published on our website, contact us at electrical@six7.lk with your request, and we will update or remove your review within 2 business days.
Do you offer incentives for leaving reviews?
No. We do not offer discounts, free services, or any incentives in exchange for reviews. We believe authentic feedback is most valuable when given voluntarily. We simply ask satisfied customers to share their honest experiences to help others make informed decisions.
How do you respond to reviews?
We respond to all reviews within 48 hours. For positive reviews, we thank customers and highlight specific aspects of their experience. For constructive feedback, we apologize for any shortcomings, explain our resolution process, and invite further private communication to address concerns. All responses are professional and solution-focused.
