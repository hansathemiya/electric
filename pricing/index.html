Emergency Electrical Repairs – 24/7 Rapid Response

For urgent electrical issues requiring immediate attention. No call-out fee – ever. You only pay for time worked and parts used.

Emergency Service Rate

Call-out fee waived
LKR 3,500 /hour
  • Minimum one hour, then billed in 30-minute increments
  • Includes diagnostic time and travel within primary service area
  • Same rate applies nights, weekends, and public holidays – no overtime surcharge
  • Call-out fee waived for all emergency repairs (LKR 1,500 value)
  • Diagnostic only fee: LKR 1,500 (waived if you proceed with repair)

When Emergency Rates Apply

  • Power outage not caused by grid issues
  • Burning smell, sparks, or smoke from electrical components
  • Circuit breaker repeatedly tripping with no obvious cause
  • Storm or flood damage affecting electrical systems
  • Partial power loss requiring urgent diagnosis
  • Electrical issues posing immediate safety risk

Example Emergency Scenarios and Estimated Time

Resetting tripped main breaker (no fault found) 30-45 min
Replacing faulty MCB or RCD 45-75 min
Diagnosing and repairing short circuit in single circuit 1-2 hours
Restoring power after minor flood damage 1.5-3 hours
Full distribution board replacement 3-5 hours

Time estimates are approximate. Actual time depends on fault complexity, accessibility, and parts availability. Your technician will provide an updated estimate upon arrival.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pricing

Why is there no call-out fee for emergencies?
We believe emergency electrical issues should not be delayed due to cost concerns. Waiving the call-out fee ensures you can call us immediately when safety is at risk. You only pay for the time we spend diagnosing and fixing the problem.
What happens if the job takes longer than estimated?
We provide time estimates based on typical scenarios, but complex faults may require additional time. If we anticipate exceeding the initial estimate, your technician will contact you for approval before continuing. You are only billed for actual time worked.
Can I get a fixed price quote instead of hourly billing?
Yes. For defined scope work like outlet installation, fixture replacement, or small projects, we can provide a fixed price quote upon request. For larger or uncertain-scope jobs, hourly billing ensures you only pay for actual work performed.
Are there extra charges for weekends or public holidays?
No. Our emergency rate applies equally 24/7, including weekends and public holidays. For planned non-emergency work scheduled on weekends, standard hourly rates apply with no surcharge.
Do you offer discounts for multiple services or repeat customers?
Yes. We offer volume discounts for multiple outlets or circuits addressed in one visit. Repeat residential customers receive priority scheduling. Commercial clients with maintenance contracts receive preferential rates on additional work.
How do I know the quote is fair compared to other electricians?
Our rates reflect licensed expertise, insured work, quality parts, and guaranteed results. We encourage you to compare not just price but value: response time, certification, warranty, and transparency. We are confident our pricing represents fair value for professional electrical service in Colombo.
What if I am not home when the technician arrives?
For emergency services, we require someone to be present for safety and access. For planned services, we can arrange key collection or access instructions in advance. No additional fee applies for pre-arranged access.
