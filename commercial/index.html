Frequently Asked Questions – Commercial Electrical Services

How do you minimize disruption to our business during electrical work?
We schedule work during off-hours, weekends, or phased periods based on your operational needs. Our technicians follow clean work practices, protect your property, and communicate daily progress. For critical systems, we plan redundant power or temporary solutions to maintain operations.
Do you handle electrical work for multi-tenant commercial buildings?
Yes. We have experience with office buildings, retail centers, and mixed-use properties. We coordinate with property managers, work within common area schedules, and ensure compliance for shared systems while serving individual tenant needs.
What compliance documentation do you provide after project completion?
We provide as-built diagrams, compliance certificates, test results for safety systems, equipment warranties, and maintenance recommendations. All documentation is suitable for insurance, regulatory, and audit purposes.
Can you work with our existing facility management or IT teams?
Absolutely. We coordinate closely with facility managers, IT departments, and other contractors to ensure seamless integration. We provide clear communication, schedule coordination, and technical documentation to support your team.
How do you price commercial electrical projects?
Commercial pricing is based on scope, complexity, timeline, and site conditions. We provide detailed written quotes with labor, materials, and timeline breakdowns. For ongoing work, we offer contract pricing with volume discounts.
Do you offer emergency support for commercial clients?
Yes. All commercial clients receive priority scheduling for emergency repairs. Preventive maintenance contract clients receive guaranteed response times and discounted emergency rates.
What industries do you have the most experience with?
We have extensive experience with retail stores, restaurants, offices, warehouses, and hospitality properties in Colombo. We also serve industrial facilities, healthcare clinics, and educational institutions. Contact us to discuss your specific industry needs.
How far in advance should we schedule commercial electrical work?
For planned projects, we recommend contacting us 2-4 weeks in advance to allow for site assessment, proposal development, and scheduling. For urgent needs or emergency repairs, we prioritize commercial clients and can often respond within 24 hours.
