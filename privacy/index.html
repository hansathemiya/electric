Privacy Policy – Six7 Electrical

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, book our services, or interact with our team.

Last Updated: March 1, 2026 Contact Us with Privacy Questions View Terms of Service →

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your personal information. If any part of this policy is unclear, please contact us before providing any personal details.

1. Introduction and Scope

1.1 This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Six7 Electrical ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you:

  • Visit our website at https://electrical.six7.lk
  • Request a quote or book our electrical services
  • Contact us via phone, email, WhatsApp, or our contact form
  • Interact with us on social media or third-party platforms
  • Participate in customer reviews or feedback programs

1.2 This Policy applies to all personal information collected through our website, services, and communications. It does not apply to information collected by third-party websites or services that we link to or that link to us.

1.3 By using our website or services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use our website or services.

1.4 This Policy is written in plain language for your understanding. If you have questions about any provision, please contact us at electrical@six7.lk before proceeding.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, physical address, or service location when you request a quote, book a service, or contact us
  • Service Details: Description of your electrical issue, property type, preferred service date/time, and any photos or documents you share
  • Payment Information: Billing details when you pay for services (processed securely through our payment partners; we do not store full credit card numbers)
  • Communication Records: Copies of emails, WhatsApp messages, call notes, or other communications with our team
  • Preferences: Marketing preferences, communication method preferences, and service feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically:

  • Website Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, links clicked, and referral sources through cookies and analytics tools
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for security and functionality
  • Location Data: Approximate location derived from IP address or precise location if you share it via WhatsApp or contact form
  • Cookie Data: Preferences and session information stored via cookies (see our Cookie Notice below)

2.3 Information from Third Parties:

  • Google Reviews: Public reviews and ratings you post on Google or other platforms
  • Referral Sources: Information from partners or affiliates who refer customers to us (with your consent)
  • Service Providers: Data from payment processors, scheduling platforms, or analytics tools we use to deliver our services

2.4 Sensitive Information:

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as health data, religious beliefs, or biometric data. If you inadvertently share such information, please contact us immediately so we can handle it appropriately.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Service Providers:

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business, including:

  • Payment processors to handle transactions securely
  • Scheduling and dispatch platforms to manage appointments
  • Website hosting and analytics providers to maintain our online presence
  • Communication tools to send you service updates via email or WhatsApp

All service providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.2 Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Six7 Electrical, our customers, or others.

4.3 Business Transfers:

If Six7 Electrical is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent website notice of any change in ownership or uses of your information.

4.4 With Your Consent:

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing, such as when you request a referral to a partner service or participate in a joint promotion.

4.5 What We Do Not Share:

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not share your service details or contact information with unrelated businesses without your consent
  • We do not use your information for purposes beyond those described in this Policy without additional notice and consent

5. Data Retention and Storage

5.1 Retention Periods:

  • Service Records: We retain customer service information for 7 years to comply with Sri Lankan business record requirements and to support warranty claims or follow-up service
  • Communication Records: We retain emails, messages, and call notes for 3 years or as needed to resolve inquiries
  • Website Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for trend analysis
  • Marketing Preferences: We retain your communication preferences until you update them or request deletion

5.2 Storage and Security:

  • Your personal information is stored on secure servers with access controls and encryption
  • Payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment partners; we do not store full credit card numbers
  • Physical records (if any) are stored in locked facilities with limited access
  • We regularly review our data retention practices to minimize holding information longer than necessary

5.3 Deletion Requests:

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at electrical@six7.lk. We will respond within 14 business days and delete your information unless we are required to retain it for legal, security, or business purposes (such as completed service records).

Learn about your right to request deletion

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction:

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information. To exercise this right, contact us at electrical@six7.lk with your name and contact details.

6.2 Communication Preferences:

  • Marketing Emails: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or contacting us directly
  • Service Communications: We may still send you essential service updates (appointment confirmations, safety notices) even if you opt out of marketing
  • Phone and WhatsApp: You may request to be contacted only via your preferred method by updating your preferences in your service booking or by contacting us

6.3 Data Portability:

Upon request, we will provide you with a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format, to the extent technically feasible.

6.4 Objection and Restriction:

You may object to or request restriction of certain processing of your information, such as direct marketing. We will honor reasonable requests unless we have compelling legitimate grounds to continue processing.

6.5 Withdrawal of Consent:

Where we process your information based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

6.6 How to Exercise Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at electrical@six7.lk with:

  • Your full name and contact information
  • A description of the right you wish to exercise
  • Any relevant details to help us locate your information

We will respond within 14 business days and may request additional verification to protect your privacy.

7. Data Security Measures

7.1 Technical Safeguards:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest where appropriate
  • Access Controls: Employee access to personal information is limited to those with a legitimate business need
  • Authentication: Secure login and multi-factor authentication for internal systems
  • Monitoring: Regular security monitoring and vulnerability assessments

7.2 Organizational Safeguards:

  • Training: Staff receive regular training on data protection and privacy practices
  • Policies: Internal policies govern how employee handle customer information
  • Contracts: Service providers sign data protection agreements requiring equivalent safeguards
  • Incident Response: We maintain procedures to detect, respond to, and notify you of any data security incident

7.3 Limitations:

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. If you believe your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately.

8.3 We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities and to help enforce this Policy by instructing their children never to provide personal information through our website or services without permission.

9.2 We may use service providers located outside Sri Lanka (such as cloud hosting or analytics platforms). When we transfer information internationally, we take steps to ensure your information receives protection consistent with this Policy and applicable Sri Lankan guidelines.

9.3 By using our services, you consent to the transfer, storage, and processing of your information in Sri Lanka and other countries where our service providers operate.

Frequently Asked Questions – Privacy Policy

Do you share my contact information with other businesses?
No. We do not sell or share your contact information with unrelated third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us deliver our services (such as payment processors or scheduling platforms), but they are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
How can I stop receiving marketing emails from you?
You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send, or by contacting us at electrical@six7.lk and requesting to be removed from our marketing list. Please note we may still send essential service communications (appointment confirmations, safety notices) even if you opt out of marketing.
How long do you keep my personal information?
We retain service records for 7 years to comply with Sri Lankan business requirements and support warranty claims. Communication records are kept for 3 years or as needed to resolve inquiries. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us, and we will delete it unless we are required to retain it for legal or business purposes.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. Payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment partners using encryption and secure protocols. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. All transactions are protected by industry-standard security measures.
Can I request a copy of the personal information you hold about me?
Yes. You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. To request a copy, contact us at electrical@six7.lk with your name and contact details. We will respond within 14 business days and provide your information in a structured, commonly used format where technically feasible.
What happens if there is a data breach affecting my information?
If we discover a security incident that compromises your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law. We will provide details about the incident, the information affected, and steps you can take to protect yourself.
Do you use cookies on your website?
Yes. We use cookies to improve website functionality, analyze usage patterns, and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Essential cookies required for website operation cannot be disabled without affecting functionality.

Cookie Notice

We use cookies and similar technologies on our website to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand where our visitors come from. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and recognize you on return visits.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, such as remembering your cookie preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about pages visited and actions taken.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices to improve your experience.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.
